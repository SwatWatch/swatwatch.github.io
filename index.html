Welcome to SwatWatch

We are a watchdog group made of students from Swarthmore and dedicated to monitoring the activities of politicians enacting and implementing the policies of Donald Trump. Our aim is to educate college students and facilitate political activism against discriminatory legislation and practices.

Our website contains both a database of legislation that has either been recently enacted, is currently being discussed, or will be proposed in the near future, as well as information on current political figures whose policies endanger or ouright oppress groups of Americans, especially minorities.
